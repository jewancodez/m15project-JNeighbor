THE DEADLINE FOR ALL ENTRIES IS May 31st, 2026 AT MIDNIGHT Fee for each additional class, known as a crossover entry, will be $60. Entry deadline is April 5th 2026. A crossover entry is entering more than one division within a category. For example, someone may enter the Novice division, if eligible, and also elect to cross over into the Open division and compete in it as well. Competitors may not participate in more than one category (Bikini, Figure, Physique, Bodybuilding, and Classic Physique) at any given show. Fees are non-refundable.
