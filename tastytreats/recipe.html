Brownies

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • Water
  • Powdered sugar
  • Unsweetened cocoa powder
  • Oil
  • Vanilla extract

Instructions

  1. Mix together dry and wet ingredients in separate bowls
  2. Combine wet and dry ingredients
  3. Pour batter into 8x8 inch baking pan lined with parchment paper
  4. Bake at 325 degrees for 40-45 min
